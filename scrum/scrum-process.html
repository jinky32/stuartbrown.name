Scrum Process

Product Vision------->Release Goal------->Sprint Goal------>User Story value

  1. Request comes in from customer. 1-1 discussion to explain process and their role in it.
  2. Development of Product Vision
  3. User Stories created (at User Story Workshop)
  4. Product Backlog Items created. Backlog groomed by Product Owner (who also sets Acceptance Criteria). Items are Estimated by the Development Team in points (rather than hours).
  5. First very draft Release Plan created based on Estimates and Product Backlog
  6. Sprint Planning takes place at Sprint Planning Meeting
    1. Sprint Goal defined
    2. User Stories broken down into Tasks by the Development Team and hours assinged to each.
  7. Sprint begins (Daily Standups)
  8. Sprint Retrospective
  9. Judge Velocity. Go back and re-examine the Release Plan
