Scrum Master

Role of the Scrum Master

Responsibilities include:

  1. Ensures that the values, practices and rules of Scrum are understood and followed.
  2. Removes impediments.
  3. Facilitates Standups
  4. Facilitiates teamwork and the relationship between the team and the Product Owner
  5. Teaches the Development Team and encourages them to improve its work, quality and productivity and teaches them to self manage.

Attributes

  1. Competence in soft skills - facilitation, moderation etc.
  2. Courageous to make necessary changes and remove impediments
  3. Present during team work hours
  4. Guarantee no deviations in process
  5. Facilitates consensus
  6. Doesn't interfere with team decisions
  7. Helps to improve the team.

Scrum Masters need to find a balance between Continuous Delivery and customer feedback versus causing continuous disruption for users.
